How To Use The App.

1. Enter a License Key

Activate the app using a license key to unlock the features of the app.

2. Filter Messages

First time app users will receive messages from ALL Group Chats(By default), users are now meant to filter messages from specific groups of their choice, by clicking on Filter Groups button.

3. Edit/Delete Groups

Users can Edit/Delete selected groups at any time by clicking on the Filter Groups button.

4. Clear Messages

You can clear current messages in the app, but it won't affect your Filtered Groups, so don't worry.

Frequently Asked Questions

JoNotify saves you the stress of having to go through bulky whatsapp group chat messages, you can just single out a specific group and a specific sender you want to be receiving messages from.

- DnD(Do not Disturb) mode must be turned off on your phone.
- The App requires Internet Connection to work properly.
- The App requires WhatsApp to be installed on the device.
- The whatsapp group must NOT be muted or archived or the app wont work properly.
- If your phone switches off, the app won't retrieve any messages, and any messages sent during the period your phone was off will not be retrieved.

Unfortunately, the app is only available for Android Phone users.

Features The App Offers.

Cloud Based

Every License Key is generated and stored in a well secured and trusted database server. Each License Key is valid for one-time use only.

Secured Privacy

JoNotify ensures that your data and messages are kept private, the app does not share private informations to any third-party services.

Filter Messages

JoNotify allows you to only get messages gotten from a specified person or contact in a WhatsApp Group Chat.
